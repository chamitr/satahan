In short....

Satahan is a notes, documents or ideas sharing website.

To search and add topics to your favourites or just to view them, go to Topics

Use checkbox tags in topics and click Search buttons to search content that belongs to the topic.

Once you have logged in,

To write new content, go to Write new

You can save your draft work and come back to it simply by clicking Draft

If you need to add new tags into a topic, please do so by clicking "Edit tags in this topic" button in "Write new" page.

Author of content has the permission to edit it. Edit and Delete options will be available to the author when content is viewed in full page mode.

Content:

Content is the back born of any website. Satahan relies on its user community to share great content. All content published on Satahan belongs to the original author of the content. When publishing content, it is important to use the correct location or topic for the content so your target audience can be reached. If you cannot find a suitable topic or tags, please feel free to create anew.

Yes, you can also attach downloadable files to your content. To add new content, go to Write new

Admin points:

Topics and tags used for content can be created, edited or deleted by user community. Admin points are designed to regulate this process. It does not mean to hinder any legitimate create, amend or delete of topics or tags. You earn admin points when you publish content, you spend them when you create, amend or delete topics or tags for your content.
